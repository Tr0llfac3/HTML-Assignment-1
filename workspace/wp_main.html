Brief Overview of the History of the Computer

  • The first Device known to carry out calculations was the abacus
    • Invented in Asia but mainly used in Babylon, China, and throughout Europe until the late middle ages
    • Used a system of sliding beads on a rack for addition and subtraction
  • In 1642, French philosopher and mathematician Blaise Pascal invented the calculating device known as the Pascaline
  • Ada Augusta is considered the first computer programmer
